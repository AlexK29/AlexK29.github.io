Purpose

How can we truly understand and form opinions in the political arena, or in any other strong cause for debate, without a fundamental grasp of the topics discussed?
Discussion and explanation are the pillars to this publication, for we do not fear unpopular views, but instead welcome them with open arms. For us at 2STAC, attaining a mass following who share common views is not the crucial objective. Instead, we aim to provide a platform where people of any background can individually assess their stance on important public matters.
